About SOCCER@KFUPM

SOCCER@KFUPM is a website to make following the results of soccer tournaments and leagues easier by providing a simple user interface and user experience. The visitors and fans will be excited to support their favorite teams when they see the results of the matches and the ranked tables of the leagues in the website. Moreover, the players fans may see the most goal scorers players.
